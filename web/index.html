Welcome to gigaflops.org

This site is dedicated to vintage supercomputers, which delivered a measured computing power of multiple GigaFLOPS up to one TeraFLOP in the past days. Our mission is to repair, maintain and preseve these supercomputers for historic reasons. Our organisation currently owns a couple of such supercomputers and most of them are dated in the 1990s, mainly Cray, SGI and SUN. During our operating times you can even access these systems and run your own workloads. Go to login and enjoy real a supercomputer from the past!
